About Me

With over two decades of experience in cybersecurity and AI security research, I specialize in securing artificial intelligence systems against emerging threats. My work focuses on adversarial machine learning, LLM security, and the development of robust defense frameworks that bridge theoretical research with practical, implementable solutions.

Having worked extensively across Fortune 500 enterprises, government agencies, and high-growth startups, I bring a rare cross-sector perspective to modern security challenges. This diverse background enables me to understand and address threats from multiple angles—whether protecting critical infrastructure, responding to sophisticated intrusions, or building security programs from the ground up.
