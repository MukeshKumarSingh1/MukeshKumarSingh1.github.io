About Me

Hello, I'm Mukesh Kumar Singh. I am currently immersed in the dynamic world of Artificial Intelligence, where my particular focus is on the rapidly evolving field of AI Security. My background spans extensive work across corporate, government, and startup environments within the cybersecurity sector. Throughout my journey, I've developed broad expertise by authoring white papers, drafting guidelines, and issuing regular cybersecurity advisories.

I hold a Bachelor’s in Computer Applications, a Master’s in Information Technology, and a Postgraduate Diploma in Artificial Intelligence. My interests bridge Cyber Security,AI Security, Data Analytics, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning with a special interest in the unique security challenges that AI introduces to modern technology systems. I am keen on exploring topics such as protecting AI models from adversarial attacks, ensuring data integrity and privacy in AI pipelines, and deploying robust security controls throughout the AI development lifecycle. At the same time I explore techniques to incorporate AI in CyberSecurity practices.

Through this platform, I aim to share my vision and experiences at the intersection of technology and security. Expect insights and projects that address contemporary issues in AI security and highlight how secure and trustworthy AI can shape our future for the better.
