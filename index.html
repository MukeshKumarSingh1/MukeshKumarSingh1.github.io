About Me

I'm a cybersecurity professional interested in AI security, working at the critical intersection of artificial intelligence and security. With a background spanning corporate, government, and startup environments, I bring a cross-functional perspective to the evolving threats facing both traditional systems and AI-driven technologies.

Over the past two decades, I've contributed to the cybersecurity field through white papers, technical guidelines, and timely security advisories. I’m deeply interested in how AI itself can be leveraged within cybersecurity: from anomaly detection to threat analysis and automation. At the same time, I actively explore the risks AI introduces to modern infrastructures, including the potential for AI-driven attacks and the misuse of generative models for malicious purposes. Exploring how to secure the future of intelligent systems—defending machine learning models from adversarial attacks, safeguarding data integrity and privacy across AI pipelines, and embedding security throughout the AI development lifecycle.

I hold a Bachelor's in Computer Applications, a Master's in Information Technology, and a Postgraduate Diploma in Artificial Intelligence. My core interests span Cybersecurity, AI Security, Data Analytics, and Machine Learning—with a particular focus on securing the fast-evolving AI ecosystem. Through this platform, I aim to share insights, research, and projects at the intersection of AI and cybersecurity. If you're interested in building secure and trustworthy AI systems or navigating the challenges posed by emerging technologies, let’s connect.
